Fuel Canister Transfer Calculator

Have too many partially used MSR canisters? Want to minimize the weight and time it takes to consolidate them?

Enter the gross weight of all your canisters below and the optimal plan will be calculated for you.

How to use the calculator

Supported sizes: MSR isobutane fuel canisters at 110g, 227g, and 450g. Gross weight = can weight + remaining fuel.

  • Type the gross weight (in grams) of each can by size. Add as many rows as you have cans.
  • The solver finds an optimal transfer plan that reduces total cans carried and empty weight.
  • See a donor/recipient graph, exact gram transfers, and total carry weight once inputs are valid.

Tip: Values lighter than an empty can or heavier than its capacity are flagged so you can correct typos quickly.
